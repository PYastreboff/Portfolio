Kotties Cleaning - A Website
for a Cleaning Company.

  • Client Kotties Cleaning
  • Category Web Design
  • Date 26 August, 2023

Kotties Cleaning approached me to create a website that would effectively showcase their work, serve as a primary contact point, and drive customer engagement. The website features detailed descriptions of their cleaning packages, a robust FAQ section, and customer reviews to build trust. I designed the homepage with striking visuals and clear navigation, ensuring visitors could easily find what they need.

Strategic calls to action throughout the site encourage visitors to book services, request quotes, and contact the company. This professional and user-friendly website has been instrumental in attracting client, enhancing Kotties Cleaning's market presence and driving business growth.
